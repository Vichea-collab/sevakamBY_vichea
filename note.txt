Based on the current architecture and features of Sevakam (a service marketplace app), you have a very solid foundation with
  Roles, Booking, Chat, KHQR Payments, and Real-time Notifications.

  To take this application to the next level—making it more engaging for users and more profitable for providers—here are my top
  recommendations for improvements and new features, broken down by category:

implement
  1. Trust & Safety (Crucial for Service Apps)
   * Provider Verification (KYC): Add a flow for providers to upload their National ID or Passport. Once verified by an admin, they
     receive a blue "Verified" badge on their profile. This drastically increases customer trust.
   * Photo Reviews: Allow customers to attach photos to their reviews when a job is completed (e.g., a photo of a repaired AC unit
     or a clean room).
   * Dispute/Resolution Center: Enhance the "Help & Support" chat into a formal ticketing system where admins can mediate disputes
     between Finders and Providers, hold funds, or issue refunds.


  2. Location & Map Features
  (I noticed you have google_maps_flutter and geolocator in your project)
   * Map-Based Search: Add a "Map View" toggle on the Search Page so Finders can visually see Providers scattered around their
     current location in Phnom Penh.
   * Live Provider Tracking: Once an order is marked as "On the Way", show the provider's live GPS location on a map to the
     customer, similar to ride-hailing apps.


  3. Enhancing the Finder (Customer) Experience
   * Favorites / Wishlist: Allow users to "Heart" or save their favorite providers. Add a "Favorites" tab so they can easily rebook
     someone they liked without searching again.
   * Multi-Language Support (L10n): Since the app uses Bakong KHQR, targeting the Cambodian market with a seamless toggle between
     English and Khmer is essential.
   * Dark Mode Support: Ensure AppTheme.dark() is fully implemented so users can toggle between Light and Dark modes for better
     nighttime viewing.


  4. Enhancing the Provider Portal
   * Earnings Dashboard & Analytics: Create a visual dashboard for providers showing their daily/weekly/monthly earnings using bar
     charts (fl_chart is great for this), along with their completion rate and average response time.
   * Advanced Calendar / Availability Blocking: Allow providers to block out specific days or hours when they are on vacation or
     busy, so they don't receive booking requests they have to decline.
   * Job Bidding System: Instead of just direct booking, allow Finders to post a "Job Request" (e.g., "Need my whole house
     painted"). Providers can then browse these open jobs and submit competitive bids/quotes.


  5. UX / Polish (Quick Wins)
   * Lottie Animations: Integrate Lottie files for empty states (e.g., an animated sleeping cat for "No messages yet") and success
     states (e.g., an animated confetti checkmark when a KHQR payment is successful).
   * Automated Review Prompts: Automatically send a push notification to the Finder 1 hour after a Provider marks an order as
     "Completed," prompting them to leave a 1-5 star rating.
